Home/About Us
Our story

Straightforward sourcing for wholesale buyers

Shukara (شكارة) is a modern commodity marketplace connecting mills, refineries, and packers with distributors and manufacturers worldwide. We hold inventory in bonded warehouses across three ports so that reservations convert into shipments in days, not months.

Every listing on this platform carries lab-verified specifications — moisture content, purity, Brix level, or acidity, depending on the commodity — so buyers can compare suppliers on facts, not marketing copy.
